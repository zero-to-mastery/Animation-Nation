The Magic Cookie

Once upon a time, a little girl found a magic cookie that granted wishes. She wished for happiness for everyone in her village. The cookie sparkled and made everyone smile forever. The end.

The Flying Bicycle

Tom discovered his old bicycle could fly when he pedaled really fast. He soared above the clouds, visited the moon, and made friends with the stars. He returned home with stardust in his pockets. The end.

The Singing Flowers

In a magical garden, flowers sang beautiful songs every morning. A lonely bird learned their melodies and became the most famous singer in the forest. Music brought all the animals together. The end.

The Kind Robot

A small robot named Beep helped everyone in the city. He fixed broken toys, helped lost pets find home, and made delicious soup for the hungry. Everyone loved their helpful robot friend. The end.
